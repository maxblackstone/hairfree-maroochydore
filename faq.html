Frequently Asked Questions

Basically, the IPL light is attracted to the pigment/melanin in the hair. The point of IPL is to shrink the hair follicle in which the hair sits, so that the hair ceases to grow. The real plus is it is virtually Pain Free!

IPL is non monochromatic (not a single wavelength) and non-focused, treats multiple follicles at once, and is attracted to varying degrees of hair colour.

IPL covers a larger area per pulse of light therefore making the process faster and with the added benefit of seeing many shades of colour.

Treatments are perfectly safe when performed with quality equipment and by an experienced therapist who holds a Laser Safety Certificate. IPL (the type of treatment we use at Hairfree Maroochydore) uses only pulsed light during the session. There is no radiation, no UV rays or nasty chemicals involved. You should avoid shady hair removal operators that use cheap machines or poorly- trained staff. It’s not worth the risk.

Hairfree Maroochydore’s IPL machine selectively targets hair follicles and therefore causes no damage to the surrounding skin. Some people experience slight localised redness immediately after the treatment which quickly dissipates.
