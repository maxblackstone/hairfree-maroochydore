Women's IPL Services

Laser Hair Removal for Women

Hairfree Maroochydore treatments are the only virtually painless IPL hair removal treatments in Australia.

Permanent hair reduction is completely safe when done with high quality equipment and experienced, qualified therapists. Because the treatment uses only light (no chemicals, radiation, etc) there’s no damage done to your skin, and only hair follicles are affected. Therapists should be experienced and must hold a laser safety certificate to be properly qualified.

We specialise in Women’s IPL Hair Removal offering treatments to the following areas:

  • Face – including Cheeks, Top Lip, Chin, Front Neck, Sideburns, Between Eyebrows, Hairline
  • Upper Body – including Underarms, Chest, Nipples, Arms, Back
  • Lower Body – including Legs, Bikini/G-bikini/Brazilian, Bottom, Feet and Toes

So, whether you’re doing your arms, legs or the most sensitive of areas, you’ll feel virtually no pain. If you’re new to hair removal, we invite you to come and try our painfree treatments with a completely free/no obligation consultation and decide for yourself.

Please note – the length of results will vary person to person. Most people will require maintenance for their hair reduction after their initial 4-6 treatments at six weekly intervals. Maintenance treatments usually start at 3 monthly intervals and increase eventually to yearly. Read our FAQs section for more information or call on 0408505876 to discuss with our therapist today.
