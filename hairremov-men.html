Men's IPL Services

Laser Hair Removal for Men

Who said permanent hair reduction was only for women? At Hairfree Maroochydore we specialise in Male specific IPL Hair Removal.

Most permanent hair reduction treatments use either a laser or intense pulsed light (IPL) to disable hair follicles and prevent the growth of hair. At Hairfree Maroochydore, we use specially-designed IPL machines which emit short pulses of light that penetrate the skin and create heat which in turn destroys the hair producing follicle. Over the course of several sessions, as most follicles in the treatment area (e.g. chest) are destroyed, you’re left with only hair free skin. This is the basis of all permanent hair reduction treatments, and virtually all areas of the body can be treated.

  • Face – including Beard, Top Lip and Hairline
  • Upper Body – including Arms, Chest, Abdomen, Shoulders, Back
  • Lower Body – including Legs

So, whether you’re doing your arms, legs or the most sensitive of areas, you’ll feel virtually no pain. If you’re new to hair removal, we invite you to come and try our painfree treatments with a completely free/no obligation consultation and decide for yourself.

Please note – the length of results will vary person to person. Most people will require maintenance for their hair reduction after their initial 4-6 treatments at six weekly intervals. Maintenance treatments usually start at 3 monthly intervals and increase eventually to yearly. Read our FAQs section for more information or call on 0408505876 to discuss with our therapist today.
